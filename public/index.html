Uploading an image on internet denatures it as it will join systems turning it into a social and monetary tool.

Onestalgo is an image sandbox, accessible on a web browser.

Users upload images and interact with them by making them grow, move, disappear within a fixed canvas changing in real time.

Uploaded without identification, the image is set free from intentions, won’t serve a community, but a collective.
Individual actions are shown as collaboration, serving the canvas to evolve
