WELCOME TO ONESTALGO


Onestalgo is a collective and evolutive canvas composed of images uploaded by users.
Take place on it !


Upload an image on the canvas, click it to grow it

You can click all the images composing the canvas

Contemplate the canvas evolving in real time.

When a third of your image reaches off the canvas, it's erased, off the database.

Click to play !

Uploading an image on internet denatures it as it will join systems turning it into a social and monetary tool.

Onestalgo is an image sandbox, framed by a web browser.

Users upload images and play with them by clicking to grow, move, disappear within a fixed canvas.

All the data being stored and updated is shown in real time, individual actions being unified to make the canvas evolve.

Uploaded without identification, the image is set free from intentions, contributing in a collective of users that has control of its data.
