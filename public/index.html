WELCOME TO ONESTALGO


Onestalgo is a collective and evolutive canvas composed by images uploaded by users.
Take place on it !


Upload an image on the canvas, click it to grow it

You can click all the images composing the canvas

Contemplate the canvas evolving in real time.

When a third of your image reaches off the canvas, it's erased, off the database.

Click to play !

Uploading an image on internet denatures it as it will join systems turning it into a social and monetary tool.

Onestalgo is an image sandbox, framed by a web browser.

Users upload images and play with them by making them grow, move, disappear within a fixed canvas changing in real time.

Uploaded without identification, the image is set free from intentions, for a collective of users.
Individual actions are shown as collaboration, serving the canvas to evolve.
