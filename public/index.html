images on internet are denatured by their context. (platform, creator, viewer, system, algorithm…)  How an environment where images exist free from their biases would be ?
Could images exist on internet without a network/system? Could social work without a network ?

Onestalgo is a SANDBOX, The box is a fixed canvas, where users bring their images as the sand. Users can shape this canvas, making these images grow, clicking on them, the click being then the shovel.
Bring your grain, free your brain
Bring your brain, free your grain

Onestalgo is an online environment accessible on a browser.
Users can upload images on a fixed canvas and make them grow until disappearance.
Uploaded without identification, the image is set free from (community) intentions, and will resonate and serve into collective playful frame.
The individuality can only be shown as collaboration, serving the purpose of making the canvas evolve.
Image for Image, Onestalgo for creation.
Onestalgo invites the user to participate or contemplate
